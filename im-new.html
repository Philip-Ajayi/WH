I'm New Here - Frequently asked questions
Here are some frequently asked questions (FAQ) about our church, who we are and what you might expect to find regarding our church family.

  • When and where are the services held?
    Join us for our uplifting services every Sunday at 8:30 AM and Thursday 6:00pm. We gather at our church located behind Accord building, Obadeyi Estate, Samonda, Ibadan.
  • What should I wear?
    We have a relaxed and inclusive dress code, where you can come as you are. Whether you prefer dressing up or opting for more casual attire, we believe that the most important thing is to come with a heart ready to encounter God and connect with others.
  • Do you have parking available?
    Yes, we have ample parking space available for visitors.
  • How long does the service last?
    Our services typically last about 120 minutes. During this time, you'll experience a vibrant worship atmosphere with contemporary music, followed by relevant and inspiring teaching from the Word of God. We also provide a time for prayer and reflection, allowing you to connect with God personally.
  • Can I bring my friends and family?
    Absolutely! We encourage you to invite your friends and family to join you for our services and events. Our church is a welcoming and inclusive space where everyone can feel accepted and loved. You can also let them know about our children and youth programs, ensuring a meaningful experience for everyone.
  • How can I get connected?
    We believe in the importance of community and offer various avenues for you to get connected. Our church has vibrant ministries and small groups where you can grow in your faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve others. We invite you to explore the different options available and find your place in our church family.
  • Are there opportunities for me to serve in the church?
    Yes, we believe that everyone has unique gifts and talents to contribute. We have various ministry teams where you can utilize your skills and passions to serve others. Whether it's in music, hospitality, children's ministry, or outreach, there's a place for you to make a difference in our church and community.
