Jesus Wants a relationship With You

For God so loved the world, that he gave his only begotten Son, that whoever believeth in him should not perish, but have everlasting life

JOHN 3:16 (KJV)

Welcome to the Path of Salvation

At Word House, we believe in the transformative power of God's love and the eternal hope that comes through accepting Jesus Christ as our Lord and Savior. This Salvation page is dedicated to sharing the profound message of salvation, guiding you towards a deeper understanding of God's plan for your life, and offering the opportunity to experience His redeeming grace. 

Understanding Salvation:

Salvation is the central theme of the Christian faith, and it represents God's incredible love for humanity. The Bible teaches us that all have sinned and fallen short of God's glory (Romans 3:23). Despite our imperfections, God offers us forgiveness and a way to be reconciled with Him through Jesus Christ (John 3:16). This act of divine grace is freely given to anyone who sincerely seeks it.

Recognizing Your Need for Salvation

It is essential to acknowledge our need for salvation and the reality of sin in our lives. Whether we have made significant mistakes or minor missteps, sin separates us from God. However, God's desire is for us to draw close to Him, experiencing His love and purpose for our lives. Recognizing the need for salvation is the first step towards experiencing God's transformative power.

The Gift of Grace

Salvation cannot be earned through our own efforts or good deeds (Ephesians 2:8-9). It is a gift of God's grace, freely offered to all who believe in Jesus Christ as the Son of God and embrace Him as their Savior. By accepting this gift, we receive forgiveness of sins and the promise of eternal life (Acts 2:21).



The Way of Salvation

Acknowledge and Confess

Take a moment to reflect on your life and acknowledge any sins or wrongdoings. Confess them before God with a repentant heart, seeking His forgiveness.

Believe in Jesus

Trust in Jesus Christ, who died on the cross for your sins and was raised to life, conquering death. Believe that He is the Son of God and the only way to salvation (John 14:6).

Accept God's Gift

Embrace God's gift of salvation with an open heart. There are no prerequisites or qualifications; it is available to everyone who believes (Romans 10:9).

Prayer of Salvation

If you are ready to accept Jesus as your Savior, you can say a simple prayer like this:

"Dear Lord Jesus, I believe that you are the Son of God and that you died for my sins. I confess my sins and ask for your forgiveness. I invite you into my heart and life to be my Lord and Savior. Thank you for your love and grace. Amen." 

What's Next?

Congratulations on taking this important step towards salvation! Your decision to follow Jesus marks the beginning of an incredible journey of faith. We encourage you to:

Read the Bible

Start with the New Testament to learn more about Jesus and His teachings.

Prayer and Worship

Communicate with God through prayer and connect with Him in worship services.

Connect with a Church Family

Join a local church where you can grow in faith, find support, and build lasting relationships with fellow believers.

Share Your Testimony

Share your newfound faith with others and be a witness to God's transforming power.

Remember, your decision for salvation is just the beginning of a life-long relationship with God. As you walk on this path of faith, always remember that God's love is steadfast, and He will guide you every step of the way.
