Faith life

Author: Babalola Adeola Blessing

Saved or unsaved, born again or not, everyone has the potential of faith; the way it’s been maximized is the difference. How? Because we are all designed to live the FAITH LIFE from inception; the evolution of man is faith-based.
But man fell from the PEAK OF FAITH to a SENSUAL LIFE (Romans 3:23). Man left God and met the serpent. The fall of man brought him down from the level of receiving and obeying instructions from God to the level of using SENSES as a determining factor in decision making. Senses are, however, appealed by appearance other than divine instructions.
However, Redemption came... "and are justified freely by his grace through the redemption that came by Jesus Christ" (Romans 2:24). We are redeemed by Christ back to what we were designed to be in the first place: to live a Faith life.
How do I live this life of faith??
It’s simple, live only by what God says because without faith, no man can please God (Hebrew 11:6a) and... "the just shall live by his faith" (Hebrew 10:38).
We are DESIGNED to live it.
We are REDEEMED to live it.
