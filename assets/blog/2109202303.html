Standing in faith

Author: Babalola Adeola Blessing

Every human being possesses two legs, purposely for balance; it's deliberately made so because it's what we need as humans to make life's journey easier. Likewise, our faith life, which is needed to stand tall, is a leg on its own, and then we need something to make it balance, and PATIENCE comes into the picture.
People that stand in faith do not draw back; the journey can't be half-journeyed, why?? FAITH DOES NOT CANCEL PROCESS.
On what, then, should I stand?
"On Christ the solid Rock I stand, all other ground is sinking sand."
There are sinking sands; assumptions, wishes are typical examples. That it works doesn't mean it's God working. It might be your assumptions. Every believer should avoid building on this sand but on the AUTHENTICITY OF THE WORD.
"Standing on the promises that cannot fail,
when the howling storm of doubt and fear assail,
by the living WORD OF GOD I SHALL PREVAIL. Standing on the promises of God"
Why the WORD?: Because that old serpent will definitely come to test the FOUNDATION OF A MAN’S FAITH, even Jesus didn’t escape it, but our Testimony is his victory.
How do I maintain my stand
  • Stand on the word. Affirm that it works because God is committed to what he says.
  • Stand and ensure you're balanced. Remember you need patience to supplement your faith. Don't get weary in the waiting room.
  • Strength: While waiting, generate strength through consistent and constant study of the word. Exercise is needed too, exercise your faith by SPEAKING WHAT YOU WANT (Prayer) and by DOING THE WORD.
  • Support: Faith supports Faith. Associate with people whose faith can help yours, especially in trying times, people on whose shoulder you can rely on. But, do not build on theirs, build a solid Faith life for yourself.
BELIEVING comes before RECEIVING. STAND ON IT and AFFIRM IT.
HAVE FAITH, SPEAK IN FAITH and most importantly STAND FIRMLY ON IT.
